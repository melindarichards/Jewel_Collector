Jewel Collector!

At the start of the game, you will see a random number labeled "number to guess." There are four jewels below the treasure chest. Each jewel is assigned a secret value that remains constant throughout the game. By clicking on a jewel, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score, which is displayed in the treasure chest.

~You win the game by matching your total score in the treasure chest to the random number.
~You lose the game if your total score in the treasure chest goes above the random number.
~Each time the game re-starts, the value of each jewel will change, as will the random number.
